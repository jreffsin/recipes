Eggplant Curry

Description

A delicate balance of spices and creamy goodness. This eggplany curry reciple is warm and hearty -- perfect for a blustery fall day. Because it uses light coconut milk instead of full fat, it fills you up without weighing you down.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium eggplant
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 can light coconut milk
  • 1 cup dry jasmine brown rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cardomom
  • 1 tsp garam marsala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Start rice simmering on stove
  2. While rice is simmering, roughly dice eggplant and green pepper
  3. Heat oil and add spices. Toast spices for 1 minute
  4. Saute eggplant and green pepper in toasted spices until tender
  5. Add coconut milk and simmer until liquid cooks down to a thick consistency
  6. Serve curry over rice
