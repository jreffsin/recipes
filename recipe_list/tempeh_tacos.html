Tempeh Tacos

Description

A sweet and spicy taco that packs a crunch. An avocado base contrasts nicely with quick pickled cabbage to create a tasty overall vegan taco experience

Ingredients

  • 1 package tempeh
  • 1/2 head of purple cabbage
  • corn tortillas
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 red onion
  • Trader Joe's Honey Aleppo Sauce
  • 1 medium avocado
  • 1 carrot
  • avocado oil

Steps

  1. Roughly dice cabbage, sprinkle with salt, and set aside to quick pickle
  2. Chop tempeh and carrots into 1" thick slices
  3. Saute tempeh and carrots in oil
  4. Add 1/2 can of black beans
  5. Once filling is hot, add generous portion of aleppo sauce. Simmer until thick
  6. Serve mixture over tortillas. Top with sliced avocado, cabbage, and finely diced red onion
