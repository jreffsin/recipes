Vegetarian Chili

Description

An easy weeknight chili with plenty of veggies, protein, and heat. The use of chipotle peppers bring a sweet spiciness to the meal that will have family and friends coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 bag frozen corn
  • 1 can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • avocado oil

Steps

  1. Roughly chop peppers and onions
  2. Saute peppers and onions in oil until tender
  3. Add can of diced tomatoes and chipotle peppers. Simmer until mixture thickens and tomatoes break down
  4. Add taco seasoning and corn. Simmer a few minutes more
  5. Serve chili hot with sliced avocado on top
